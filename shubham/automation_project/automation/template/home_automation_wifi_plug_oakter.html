Oakplug Mini (WiFi)

Automate your low power appliances. Connect it to your Oakter App and schedule, time and remotely turn your devices on and off. Low power appliances include Coffee making machine, Cooler, Speakers, Lamps, Motor (less than 2HP), TV, Aquarium etc.

Specifications 

  • Supports upto 1.3KW at 230V
  • Range: 110V - 290V
  • Highly conductive copper contacts
  • BS 546 Type D pin configuration to fit standard Indian small wall sockets
  • Universal output socket
  • Plug and play device, requires no installation
  • Polycarbonate enclosure
  • Firmware designed to handle frequent power outages
  • Constantly evolves with Over The Air firmware updates
